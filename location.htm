How to find us:

Our full postal address is: ‘Scales’, Berrier, Penrith, Cumbria. CA110XE.

From the M6 junction 40, take the A66 to Keswick. After about 8 miles, turn RIGHT at The Sportsman Inn (signposted Hutton Roof), then turn LEFT behind The Sportsman Inn (signposted Hutton Roof). Continue, passing through the hamlet of Berrier, then downhill for about a further 3miles. At the crossroads, approx 3½ miles from the A66, take a RIGHT turn (signposted Newsham/Lamonby.) Continue for 1 mile. Scales is on the left BEFORE the bungalow. Enter the drive and turn immediately left to the campsite.
